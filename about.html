Introducing MLBlocks

MLBlocks was co-founded by Rishabh Anand and Sarvasv Kulpati, for the NTUitive Ideasinc 2019 startup competition. MLBlocks earned the title of Most Innovative Startup and won SGD10000 in seed money.

Machine Learning is difficult to learn and implement. It's even worse when you don't have access to appropriate hardware. At MLBlocks, we're all about simplicity. We provide a unified platform to build, train, and deploy high-performance, high-accuracy models on the cloud without the hassle.
