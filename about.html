Introducing MLBlocks

MLBlocks was founded by Rishabh and Sarvasv, as part of NTUitive Ideasinc 2019 startup competition. MLBlocks earned the title of "Most Innovative Startup" and won SGD10000 in seed money.

Machine Learning is difficult to learn and implement. It's even worse if you don't have access to appropriate hardware. At MLBlocks, we're all about simplicity. We provide a unified platform for building, training and deploying models on the cloud without the hassle.
